  • Water-based stains

Polished Concrete

Achieve a mirror-like, high-gloss finish that is both beautiful and incredibly durable — the premium flooring choice for modern spaces.

  • High-gloss finish
  • Low maintenance
  • Extremely durable
  • Reflective surface

Epoxy Floors

High-performance epoxy coating systems that protect and beautify concrete surfaces — ideal for garages, warehouses, and commercial facilities.

  • Metallic finishes
  • Flake systems
  • Quartz broadcast
  • Chemical resistant

Metallic Epoxy

Create a stunning, one-of-a-kind floor with metallic pigments that produce a flowing, three-dimensional effect unlike any other flooring system.

  • Unique patterns
  • 3D visual depth
  • Custom colors
  • Seamless surface

Flake & Quartz Systems

Decorative vinyl flake and quartz aggregate broadcast systems deliver texture, slip resistance, and vibrant color — perfect for high-traffic areas.

  • Slip resistant
  • Heavy traffic rated
  • Hundreds of blends
  • UV stable colors
